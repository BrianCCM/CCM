Founder

Dalton Hunt

Dalton Hunt is the founder of Elevated Construction and Development. With over 13 years of experience in the industry, Dalton has established himself as a highly skilled and visionary builder. Throughout his career, he has built or renovated over 100 homes, showcasing his expertise in luxury home construction and renovations. He also built multiple business centers, student housing facilities, and multi-family units.

Dalton's passion for creating exceptional living environments is evident in his meticulous attention to detail and commitment to quality craftsmanship. He works closely with clients, architects, and designers to bring their unique visions to life, ensuring that each project reflects the individuality and desires of the homeowner.

As the driving force behind Elevated Construction and Development, Dalton emphasizes the importance of exceptional customer service, open communication, and delivering projects on time. His hands-on approach and deep industry knowledge ensure that every aspect of the construction process is executed with precision and to the highest standards.

With a reputation built on trust and excellence, Dalton Hunt continues to be a respected figure in the field of luxury home building and renovations.
