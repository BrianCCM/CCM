# Marketing Audit: Cedar Ridge Plumbing
**URL:** https://www.cedarridgeplumbing.com
**Date:** 2026-04-27
**Business Type:** Local Service Business (Plumbing) — Cedar City, UT
**Overall Marketing Score: 48/100 (Grade: D)**

---

## Executive Summary

Cedar Ridge Plumbing (also operating publicly as "Same Day Plumbing") is a 30+ year, family-owned plumbing company run by owner Mike Steele, based at 3588 W Benson Way, Cedar City, UT. The site is built on Wix and serves as a basic digital business card — it covers the essentials (services, contact info, service areas) but executes weakly on the marketing fundamentals that drive lead conversion in a competitive local trades market.

The single biggest issue is brand identity confusion. The site is titled "Cedar Ridge Plumbing" but pages, email (samedayplumb@gmail.com), and the Google Maps listing all reference "Same-Day-Plumbing." A second domain (samedayplumbutah.com) appears to mirror the same business. This dual identity dilutes review accumulation, fragments local SEO authority, and likely confuses customers searching for either name. NAP consistency across citations is poor.

The second major issue is social proof. Despite operating for 30+ years, the homepage shows zero testimonials, zero star ratings, and no embedded review widget. Competitors are running circles around this — Iron Mountain Plumbing has 500+ five-star reviews surfaced prominently, Roto-Rooter Cedar City has 104 reviews at 4.9 stars, and Imlay Plumbing has 173 reviews at 4.8 stars. Cedar Ridge is invisible in the review economy that dominates how Cedar City homeowners pick a plumber.

Conversion mechanics are also weak: no homepage form, no instant-quote tool, no after-hours chat, vague hours ("Mon-Fri 9-5" alongside "Available 24/7" — which is it?), and a generic 5-field contact form on a separate page. SEO is bare bones — no schema markup, missing meta description, no city-specific landing pages despite serving 8+ towns, and at least two referenced pages return 404s (/about, /contact). The site title and H1 are decent, the value prop ("Trusted Same Day Plumber in Cedar City") is clear, and the phone CTA is visible — those are the bright spots.

For Cedar City Marketing, this is a high-fit prospect: established business, motivated owner, clear competitive pressure from better-marketed rivals, and many fixable problems that can produce visible lift in 30-60 days.

## Quick Wins
*Implement this week. Low effort, immediate impact.*

1. **Fix the brand name conflict.** Pick one — "Cedar Ridge Plumbing" or "Same Day Plumbing" — and make every touchpoint match (email, GBP, website footer, Yelp). Right now reviews are split between two GBP/Yelp listings under "Same-Day-Plumbing." Impact: **High**.
2. **Embed Google Reviews on the homepage.** Use a free Wix widget (Elfsight, EmbedSocial) to show live review feed + star count above the fold. With 30 years in business they almost certainly have reviews to surface — they're just hidden. Impact: **High**.
3. **Fix the 404s on /about and /contact.** The navigation links to these pages but they return 404 (only /copy-of-about and /contact-us work). This is a credibility and SEO killer. Impact: **High**.
4. **Resolve the hours contradiction.** The site says both "Mon-Fri 9am-5pm" and "Available 24/7." Pick one framing — recommend "24/7 emergency service, office hours Mon-Fri 9-5." Impact: **Med**.
5. **Add a meta description to every page.** Right now the homepage has none. Write a 150-character pitch with phone number and "Cedar City plumber" keyword. Impact: **Med**.
6. **Add LocalBusiness/Plumber schema (JSON-LD).** Wix supports this via custom code injection. Include name, address, phone, hours, service area, aggregate rating once reviews are connected. Impact: **High** for local pack visibility.
7. **Add a homepage form** above the fold or sticky on mobile. 5 fields → 3 fields (Name, Phone, Issue). Most local-service traffic converts on form, not on /contact-us. Impact: **High**.
8. **Add image alt text** sitewide — Wix images currently rely on default filenames. Use descriptors like "Cedar City water heater installation by Mike Steele." Impact: **Med** for SEO.
9. **Add a "Text us" button.** Same number, sms: link. Trade customers under 50 prefer text over call. Impact: **Med**.
10. **Display the license number and "Licensed & Insured"** badge in the header/footer. Currently no Utah plumbing license is displayed anywhere — this is table-stakes trust signal that competitors all show. Impact: **Med**.

## Strategic Recommendations
*Implement this month. Medium effort, compounding impact.*

1. **Build city-specific landing pages** for each of the 8 service-area cities (Cedar City, Enoch, Parowan, St. George, Washington, Toquerville, New Castle, Hurricane). 600-800 words each, unique testimonials, local-specific imagery. Currently one shared page = lost ranking on every neighboring-city search. Impact: **High**.
2. **Build dedicated service pages with depth** for Water Heater Replacement, Drain Cleaning, Emergency Plumbing, Re-Pipes, Sewer. Each should target a distinct keyword cluster, include FAQ schema, before/after photos, typical pricing range. Impact: **High**.
3. **Run a 90-day review-acquisition push.** Text every customer post-job with a Google review link. With 30 years of customers, even targeting the last 12 months of jobs should add 50-100+ reviews. Goal: 100 reviews in 90 days. Impact: **High**.
4. **Migrate off Wix or upgrade plan to enable proper SEO and speed.** Wix limits schema control, page-speed tuning, and URL structure. If staying on Wix, at minimum upgrade to remove the "Website by Walt & Gordon" footer credit, which reads amateur. Impact: **Med**.
5. **Photo and video shoot.** Currently one photo of Mike. Need: trucks with logo, team in branded shirts, a real install in progress, before/after shots. This single asset upgrade transforms perceived professionalism. Impact: **High**.
6. **Set up call tracking** (CallRail or similar). Without it, there's no way to attribute leads to channels, which means marketing spend will be guessed. Impact: **High** for measurement.
7. **Launch Google Local Service Ads (LSA).** Cedar City plumbing LSA inventory is undersaturated. With Google Guarantee badge, leads are pay-per-lead, fits a small operator's cash flow. Impact: **High**.

## Long-Term Initiatives
*This quarter and beyond. Bigger lifts, durable advantage.*

1. **Build a content engine** — 2 blog posts/month answering Cedar City plumbing questions ("Why does my water heater pop in winter Cedar City?", "Hard water solutions for Iron County homes"). Target long-tail informational keywords competitors aren't writing about. Impact: **Med-High** over 6-12 months.
2. **Launch a maintenance membership program** — $15-25/month for annual flush, drain inspection, priority emergency response. Recurring revenue smooths cash flow and builds loyalty moat. Iron Mountain and Roto-Rooter both have these. Impact: **High** on LTV.
3. **Build a referral program** with trackable links — $50 credit for both referrer and referee. With 30 years of customers, referral is likely already the #1 source — formalizing it doubles output. Impact: **Med**.
4. **Establish brand consolidation strategy** — decide if "Same Day Plumbing" is retired in favor of "Cedar Ridge Plumbing" or vice versa, then 301-redirect the losing domain, claim and merge both GBPs (or close one), and rebuild citation consistency from scratch. Impact: **High** on long-term SEO.

## Detailed Analysis by Category

### Content & Messaging — 55/100
The homepage value proposition is clear: "Trusted Same Day Plumber in Cedar City, Utah. 30+ Years of Honest Plumbing." Headline is benefit-led, mentions the geo, and signals speed. Service list is comprehensive. However: the body copy is generic ("Plumbing Problems? We Get It."), there are zero customer testimonials displayed anywhere on the homepage, no specific job stories, no "as seen on" or local press mentions, and service descriptions on /services are 1-2 sentences each. Depth is shallow vs. competitors. The 30-year claim is the strongest asset and is underleveraged — it should anchor the hero, not appear as an H2 mid-page.

### Conversion Optimization — 45/100
**Working:** Phone number is in the header with click-to-call, "Contact Now" button repeats throughout, free estimates promise is stated.
**Broken or missing:** No homepage form (every form is a click away on /contact-us), 5-field form is 1-2 fields too long for a local trades inbound, no sticky mobile CTA, no chat, no SMS option, no after-hours messaging beyond "Available 24/7," no urgency mechanics (no "next available appointment," no "book online," no live availability). Trust signals near the form are weak — no badge, no rating, no guarantee restated.

### SEO & Discoverability — 35/100
**On-page:** Title tag is decent. No meta description. H1 is solid. No JSON-LD schema (LocalBusiness, Plumber, AggregateRating, FAQPage all missing). Image alt text appears default. Internal linking is okay but unstructured.
**Site structure:** /about and /contact return 404s while /copy-of-about and /contact-us work — classic Wix migration debt. The "/copy-of-about" URL alone signals an unfinished site.
**Local SEO:** NAP appears consistent on the website but the brand split between "Cedar Ridge Plumbing" and "Same-Day-Plumbing" fragments citations across Yelp, BBB, GBP, Alignable, Wheree. GBP review count for Cedar Ridge specifically is essentially nonexistent in search results, while the older "Same-Day-Plumbing" listing appears more prominent. No city-specific pages despite 8 service areas.
**Speed/tech:** Wix sites typically score 50-70 on PageSpeed mobile — likely a drag here.

### Competitive Positioning — 40/100
Cedar City plumbing has clear leaders by social proof:

| Competitor | Reviews | Rating | Positioning |
|---|---|---|---|
| Iron Mountain Plumbing | 500+ (some sources cite 665) | 4.6-5.0 | "Local & Family Owned, Same-Day Service" — most aggressive marketing |
| Imlay Plumbing | 173 | 4.8 | Established, residential focus, fair pricing |
| Roto-Rooter Cedar City | 104 | 4.9 | National brand, 24/7, free estimates |
| Mr. Rooter Cedar City | Active GBP | n/a (national) | National franchise, paid ads heavy |
| Cedar City Plumbing | ~1 visible | 5.0 | Newer, owner Matt Graham, "3 generations" angle |
| **Cedar Ridge Plumbing** | **Unclear/split** | **"5-star" claimed** | **30 years, "honest pricing"** |

Cedar Ridge has a real positioning angle (longest-tenured, owner-led, "honest pricing") but isn't winning the visibility war because the social proof isn't surfaced. Iron Mountain literally uses "Same-Day Service" as a marketing line — Cedar Ridge owns that domain (samedayplumbutah.com) but is letting Iron Mountain own the search positioning.

### Brand & Trust — 55/100
The about page (only accessible via /copy-of-about) tells a real story: Mike Steele, Arizona State finance grad, started Same Day Plumbing to support his family, 30+ years experience. That's a great foundation. What's missing: a real headshot done professionally, a team page, photos of the work van, the Utah plumbing license number, BBB accreditation status (not visible), trade association memberships (PHCC?), warranty/guarantee specifics, Mike's personal phone or text option. The "5-star reviews" claim with no count or proof reads as marketing copy, not evidence.

### Growth & Strategy — 50/100
No email capture mechanism on the site. No newsletter, no maintenance plan, no service membership. No lead magnet (e.g., "Cedar City Hard Water Guide PDF"). No upsell paths visible — water heater install doesn't cross-sell whole-home water filtration, drain clearing doesn't pitch annual maintenance. No clear referral program. 30 years of customer relationships are not being systematically harvested. Site has no blog, no video, no social channels visibly linked.
